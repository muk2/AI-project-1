Line1  represents the start point 
Line 2 represents the end point 
Line 3 represents the dimensions
After the lines represent the dimensions on the grid
0 means open and 1 means closed

How to see h-value, g-value, f-value:
click on the vertex you want to check on the grid and the values will appear on the command line.
